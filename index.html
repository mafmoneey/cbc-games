Hosts

The hosts are responsible for welcoming each participating school, guiding teams throughout the event, and ensuring organization and a positive experience. They represent the spirit of CBC Games through leadership, responsibility, and teamwork.

Our History

CBC Games was created to bring together schools through sports, promoting values such as respect, teamwork, and fair play. Over the years, it has grown into a meaningful tradition that creates unforgettable experiences for all participants.
